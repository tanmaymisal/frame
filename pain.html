PAIN 

Nagato (長門, Nagato) was a shinobi of Amegakure and descendant of the Uzumaki clan.
     Forming Akatsuki alongside his friends (and fellow war orphans) Yahiko and Konan,
     Nagato dreamed of bringing peace to the violent shinobi world.
     However, following Yahiko's death, Nagato adopted the alias of Pain (ペイン, Pein) and, along with Konan, began leading a
     new Akatsuki — one that would force the world into peace using any means necessary.
